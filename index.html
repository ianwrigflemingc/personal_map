The Journey to Our New Home

Anyone who has moved knows there are a million details to executing it right. There are many little details, like finding boxes and buying packing tape. Then there are the big items, like finding, renting or buying a home. This site highlights only the physical execution of our move. The maps below highlight the routes travelled, the places stayed, arrival and departure times over the course of the four days needed to complete the move. The approximate distance travelled to accomplish the move was approximately 6000 kms. Details of the trip are contained in each individual map.
